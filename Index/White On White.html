White On White

Bryan Cranston is known for his iconic roles in Breaking Bad and Malcolm in the Middle, but in Kith's 2023 Spring Delivery I, he's showcasing a different kind of performance: fashion. In the latest campaign, Cranston is sporting head-to-toe white, paired with Adidas Samba shoes, and it's a look that's making waves.

The campaign features Cranston in a variety of settings, from walking down the street to lounging on a rooftop. But no matter the backdrop, the all-white ensemble stands out. From a crisp white tee to tailored white pants, every piece is perfectly tailored to Cranston's lean physique. It's a look that's understated but confident.

"I love the simplicity and purity of white," Cranston said in an interview with Kith. "It's a color that can be calming and serene, but also bold and striking. It's the perfect canvas for any outfit, and I wanted to experiment with that for this campaign."

"The collection itself is a study in minimalism, with clean lines and classic silhouettes taking center stage. But it's Cranston's interpretation of the all-white look that's stealing the show. He wears a range of garments from the collection, including a white crewneck sweatshirt, a white button-up shirt, and white cargo pants.

Of course, the Adidas Samba shoes are the perfect finishing touch. With their classic design and crisp white colorway, they tie the entire look together. "I've always been a fan of Adidas," Cranston said. "Their designs are timeless, and the Samba is no exception. Plus, they're so comfortable."

But it's not just about the clothes and shoes. For Cranston, fashion is a way of expressing himself and connecting with others. "When you wear something that makes you feel good, it shows," he said. "And when people see that, it can be a conversation starter. It's a way of connecting with people, even if you don't say a word."

Cranston's love of fashion and style is evident in his approach to the campaign. He's not just wearing the clothes, he's embodying them. "I feel like a blank slate," Cranston said. "There's something liberating about shedding all the colors and patterns and just focusing on the simplicity of white. It's a look that's understated but confident."

For Kith, the campaign is a celebration of simplicity and timeless style. "We wanted to create a collection that was versatile and effortless," said Ronnie Fieg, Kith's founder. "And who better to showcase that than Bryan Cranston? He's a master of his craft, and he brings that same level of dedication and expertise to fashion."

So if you're looking to make a statement this spring, take a page from Bryan Cranston's book and go all white. It's a look that's timeless, effortless, and undeniably cool. "Fashion should be fun," Cranston said. "It's not about following trends or rules. It's about finding what makes you feel good and owning it." With Kith's Spring Delivery I, you can do just that.
